Welcome

My name is Joseph Baena. I am a Computer Science major interested in the intersection of human-computer interaction, artificial intelligence, and music. I am an avid pianist, and have been studying piano and music for 15 years. I have also recently taken up social dancing as a new activity to explore the symbiotic nature of music and movement. Outside of the classroom, I am currently the Resident Computer Consultant in Kimball Hall, Stanford's Arts Theme House, and will be returning as a Resident Assistant next year.

Recordings

An Evening of Scott Joplin





Last February, I performed a diverse selection of some of my favorite piano rags, waltzes, and character pieces of African-American composer Scott Joplin at an Arts Salon at Kimball Hall. This event was sponsored by ResArts under the direction of James Cadena.
